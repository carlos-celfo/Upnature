Some tips to make your daily life more sustainable:

  1. Drink from reusable bottles.
  2. Unplug the charger once your devices like your phone or laptop are fully charged.
  3. Use your bike or walk to visit your friends.
  4. Recycle your waste.
  5. Share things like books with your friends.
  6. Visit flea markets. Besides adding to sustainability, this can also be a fun activity for you and your friends.
